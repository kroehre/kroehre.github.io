Juicebox

In order to more effectively test landing pages for paid search keywords, Acumen needed a tool to rapidly create landing pages that varied in verbiage and basic design (colors, font-sizes, etc). Using a simple templating syntax, a base template can be maintainably created with any number of configurable variables. Variations of the template can then be made with minimal effort and assigned a unique URL.

Chorus CMS

Chorus enables management of content in-context, maximizing flexibility without exceeding the bounds of a website's design. Chorus employs a content version control system (inspired by Git and Mercurial), allowing specific content changes to be published and reversed. This enables teams of content managers to work in parallel on the same content with minimal risk of affecting each other's changes. As the creator and primary developer, I helped teams with its implementation and ongoing improvements.
